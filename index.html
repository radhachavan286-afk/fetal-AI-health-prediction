Why Fetal Health Monitoring Matters

94% Preventable
UN SDG Aligned

Reduction of child mortality is reflected in several of the United Nations' Sustainable Development Goals and is a key indicator of human progress.

The UN expects that by 2030, countries will end preventable deaths of newborns and children under 5, aiming to reduce under-5 mortality to at least 25 per 1,000 live births.

Maternal mortality accounts for 295,000 deaths during and following pregnancy and childbirth (as of 2017). The vast majority (94%) occurred in low-resource settings.

Cardiotocograms (CTGs) are a simple, cost-accessible option to assess fetal health, shedding light on Fetal Heart Rate (FHR), fetal movements, uterine contractions, and more.
